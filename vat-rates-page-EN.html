Common Questions

VAT & GST Reference FAQ

Where does this data come from?

The rates and filing information are compiled by VAT Support from official government tax authority websites and verified publications, current as of early 2026.

Why does the US show a range for standard rate?

The United States has no federal VAT. Sales and use taxes are imposed at state and local levels, with rates and rules varying significantly by jurisdiction. State rates range from 2.9% (Colorado) to 7.25% (California), plus additional local taxes.

Does Canada have one GST rate?

Federal GST is 5%, but provinces may add HST (combined 13-15%), QST (Quebec 9.975%), or PST (BC 7%, Manitoba 7%, Saskatchewan 6%). Always check the applicable provincial rate.
