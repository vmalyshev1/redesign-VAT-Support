Why register for VAT?

If you're selling goods or services in a foreign country and exceed certain thresholds, you're legally required to register for VAT. Non-compliance can result in penalties, back-taxes, and even being barred from selling in that market.

Did you know?

Many businesses are unknowingly non-compliant. Selling on Amazon, eBay, or your own website to EU customers? You may already have VAT obligations in multiple countries.
