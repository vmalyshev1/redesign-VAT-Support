Our story

VAT.support was founded by tax professionals who saw firsthand how complex cross-border VAT had become — and how underserved growing businesses were. Traditional accounting firms charged premium rates for basic compliance, while DIY solutions left companies exposed.

We built VAT.support to be different: expert-led services at transparent prices, powered by technology that keeps you compliant without the complexity. Today, we help thousands of businesses navigate VAT in over 50 countries.

Our Mission

To remove VAT complexity as a barrier to international growth. Every business should be able to sell globally without worrying about tax compliance.
